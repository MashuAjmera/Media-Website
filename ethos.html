What is Media Cell and what role does it play?

18 Sep, 2016

The Media Cell since its inception, remains more a platform of polishing skills of oration, creation, curation and developing a better perspective towards life in general. The cell functions not just in a way adhering to its name but tends to create an all rounded approach with perfection in events, workshops and internal as well as external competitions spread throughout the year.

There comes to be more stress on 'how', with an obvious planning of 'what'. From the time of its creation the primary aim in every activity remains the art/concept/motive of learning something out of it. It was formed in 2016 as a joint initiative by Yash Agarwal, PV Sindhuri and Jeetendra Ajmera. Since then, the club has grown exponentially and in 2017, they organised TEDx LNMIIT, along with a whole lot of other initiatives.

Roles

The Media Cell is the media body of LNMIIT who have their eyes and ears everywhere, looking to grasp the most important information; even from the most obscure events. Along with the singularly given responsibility of training anchors and hosts for all the events around the college, the Media Cell calls upon external guidance and internal sessions that train students in general communication and soft skills.

Hosting a plethora of flagship events every year has become a part and parcel of the Media Cell along with what it is supposed to do, all credits to the broad vision that it was incepted with. Its events range from innovative sessions of motivational and idea oriented talks, to Duologue, an event that emphasizes the importance of interaction with new people through and innovative way.

The best thing to know: is to know everything.

Along with these, the Media Cell believes in the power of stories and creating them henceforth. From training and development to event organization and curation, we as a family have created powerful narratives that generate awe in our very own minds.

The Difference

Well scripting it in an introductory mail itself is some sort of task. However, a major feature that we confidently brag of is the TEAM we possess. Every single bit of time that we spend together, it feels like family learning together, with no seniority whatsoever, J . We all enjoy doing what we are. The very fact that we have a team that is dedicated, creative and patient only adds to the overall charm, making every moment spent together memorable. Our monthly meetings end up with melodious guitar sessions or may be poetry-story narrations, pretty often than not which makes even the meetings an irresistible fun!
