Quizzinga

Quizzinga was found with the sole motive of inculcating a decent quizzing culture in LNMIIT. It started as an independent body but was later accomodated in the Science & Tech Council. The club was started by Dheeraj Yalamanchi of Y09 with 3 other members. They believe that “knowledge is a type of social fodder which is feeding the information-driven world of today; and things like quizzing help a lot in increasing one’s knowledge and it also grooms the overall personality of an individual”. They have represented LNMIIT in numerous quiz competitions and have won most of them. Their performances at BITS Pilani – India Quiz, TATA Crucible and IIM Lucknow’s Business Quiz are few of their remarkable achievements. They have successfully organized many quizzing events during Plinth and Vivacity which were a big success. In the year 2015, they had Mr Kunal Savarkar (KBC Content head) as a host in their quiz fest. In 2011 they started their FB Group and page where they took quizzing to a new level and they are getting an enormous response from all the parts of the country. Abiding by the above quote Quizzinga has achieved a lot in all these years and all the credit goes to the hardworking team of the club. Their members have been travelling across the length and breadth of the nation to participate in various prestigious quizzes.


Some of their achievements include :


Tata Crucible Internationals Runners-up 2015
Tata Crucible Nationals Runners-up 2015
Tata Crucible Regional Winners In 2012, 2013, 2015, 2016, 2017
Jaipuria Institute Business Quiz Winners 2014
MNIT ‘s India Quiz & Business Quiz Winners 2016
