ASME

We at ASME LNMIIT try to guide its members by conducting events, workshops and floating projects which are guided by some of the faculty members.

The students of ASME LNMIIT Student Section participate in various competitions held all over India. The events comprise of major competitions of HPVC (Human Powered Vehicle). It was the first attempt of the students of the institute in 2017 and the team in its first trial, cleared all the rounds of the contest.

In the ASME e-fest held this year at Delhi, the Vehicle was named Cross-bow and its design and initial report was selected in top 40 designs, the next four rounds were cleared at the venue with 17th rank in the final round. The team which was behind the Vehicle and its performance comprised of 17 members, 6 of whom were from Y-16 batch and rest from Y-15 batch. The model scored most in stability and safety measures. Team leaders were Jatin Bhandari and Shashank from Y-15 and drivers were Vijay Rathore, Harsh Bangur and Saumya from Y-15 with Mudit Parnami as the presenter.

Another competition in the E-fest was Old Guard Presentation Competition in which a student of LNMIIT, Harsh Vardhan Gupta of Y-15 batch secured 2nd Rank. The Old Guard Poster Competition also ended with Saumya of Y-15 as 5th Rank holder who made Nano Fluid Pipe’s design.
